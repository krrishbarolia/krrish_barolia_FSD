Shaniwar Wada

  • Shaniwar Wada is a historical fortification in the city of Pune.
  • Built in 1732, it was the seat of the Peshwas of the Maratha Empire until 1818 when the Peshwas lost control to the British East India Company.
  • The fort itself was largely destroyed in 1828 by an unexplained fire, but the surviving structures are now maintained as a tourist site.
