  • Parvati Hill is a hillock in Pune that houses the Parvati Temple, one of the oldest heritage structures in Pune.
  • The temple offers panoramic views of the city and is a popular spot for tourists and locals alike.
  • The hill has 103 steps that lead up to the temple, making it a peaceful and spiritual journey for many visitors.
